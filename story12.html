The Door That Should Never Be Opened

By Admin | April 2026

It started as a completely ordinary evening. The rain tapped softly against the windows, and the house felt unusually quiet.

At first, she ignored it. Maybe it was just the unfamiliar environment. But as the hours passed, she began to notice a faint sound coming from upstairs.

"Sometimes, the things we ignore are the ones we should fear the most."

Curiosity slowly turned into unease. The sound continued — slow, deliberate, and impossible to explain.

She walked toward the staircase, each step echoing louder than expected. At the end of the hallway, a door stood slightly open.

When she finally pushed it open, the room was empty. But just as she stepped inside, the sound started again… behind her.
