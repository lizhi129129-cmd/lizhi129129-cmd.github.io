The Voice From the Basement

By Admin | April 2026

The house had been empty for years, or at least that’s what everyone said.

But on the first night, she heard a voice calling her name from the basement.

"Some places remember more than we do."

At first, she thought it was her imagination. But the voice came again.

Slowly, she walked down the stairs. Each step felt heavier than the last.

When she reached the bottom, the voice stopped.

And then, something whispered right behind her ear.
